Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at Lehigh University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Luis Nunes Vicente and participate in the Optimization and Machine Learning (OptML) group.

My research interests are Optimization for Machine Learning. You can find a recent version of my CV here.

I grew up in Hanoi, Vietnam, and earned a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Education from Hanoi National University of Education in 2019. I then spent one year in graduate studies at the Institute of Mathematics in Vietnam before pursuing my PhD at Cornell University’s School of Operations Research and Information Engineering (ORIE). I obtained an MS degree from Cornell in August 2023 and completed my PhD in 2025 under the guidance of Prof. Katya Scheinberg (now at the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Tech) and Dr. Lam M. Nguyen at IBM Research.

During my PhD, I completed research internships at IBM Research (Thomas J. Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY) in summer 2022 and 2023, mainly advised by my mentor Lam M. Nguyen. In summer 2024, I received the Siegel Family Endowment PiTech PhD Impact Fellowship to work at ADAPT Community Network (New York City, NY).
