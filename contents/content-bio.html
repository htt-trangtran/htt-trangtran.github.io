Biography

I am a second-year PhD student in the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering ORIE, Cornell University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Katya Scheinberg at Cornell ORIE and Dr. Lam Nguyen at IBM Research, Thomas J. Watson Research Center.
My research interest is Applied Maths, particularly in Optimization for Machine Learning. You can find a recent version of my CV here.
I grew up in Hanoi, Vietnam. I got my Bachelor degree in Mathematics and Education at Hanoi National University of Education. Then I spent one year for graduate study at the Institute of Mathematics in Vietnam and later moved to Cornell University for my PhD.
