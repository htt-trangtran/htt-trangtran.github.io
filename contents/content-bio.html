Biography

I am a second-year PhD student in the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering ORIE, Cornell University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Katya Scheinberg at Cornell ORIE and Dr. Lam Nguyen at IBM Research, Thomas J. Watson Research Center.
My research interest is Optimization for Machine Learning and other Applications. You can find a recent version of my CV here.
I grew up in Hanoi, Vietnam. I got my Bachelor degree in Mathematics and Education at Hanoi National University of Education. Then I spent one year for graduate study at the Institute of Mathematics in Vietnam and later moved to Cornell University for my PhD. In summer 2022, I am excited to be an AI Research Intern at IBM Research (Thomas J. Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY). My mentor Lam Nguyen advises me on a project about time series models and related machine learning applications.
