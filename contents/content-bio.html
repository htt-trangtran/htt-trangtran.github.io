Biography

I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering ORIE, Cornell University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Katya Scheinberg (now she is at the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Tech) and Dr. Lam M. Nguyen at IBM Research, Thomas J. Watson Research Center.
My research interests are Optimization for Machine Learning and Time Series. You can find a recent version of my CV here.
I grew up in Hanoi, Vietnam. I got my Bachelor degree in Mathematics and Education at Hanoi National University of Education. Then I spent one year for graduate study at the Institute of Mathematics in Vietnam and later moved to Cornell University for my PhD. I obtained an MS degree from Cornell in August 2023. In summer 2022 and 2023, I was fortunate to do AI research internships at IBM Research (Thomas J. Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY), mainly advised by my mentor Lam M. Nguyen. In summer 2024, I was lucky to receive the Siegel Family Endowment PiTech PhD Impact Fellowship to work at ADAPT Community Network (New York City, NY), advised by Ronak Parikh.
