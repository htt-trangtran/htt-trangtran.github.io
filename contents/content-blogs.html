My first paper about SMG algorithm was accepted to ICML 2021!
I'm fortunate to be advised by my mentor Lam Nguyen, who has supported me doing research from the very first beginning steps. Without his endless passion and valuable advice, I wouldn't be able to publish this paper in my first year. This project has become a wonderful journey for me. Though I and Lam never had a chance to meet in person (due to the pandemic), we still enjoy every moments in this journey together.
